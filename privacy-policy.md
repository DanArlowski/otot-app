# Privacy Policy for Otot

**Effective Date:** April 23, 2026
**Last Updated:** April 23, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Otot ("the App") handles information in connection with your use of the App. Otot is developed and operated by Dan Arlowski, an individual developer based in Israel ("Developer", "I", "my").

Otot is an application designed to support Jewish religious practice. I respect your privacy and have intentionally designed the App to minimize data collection. This policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

**Contact:** info@otot-siddur.com

## Summary (Plain Language)

- I do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any server.
- All data, including your location, stays on your device.
- There are no user accounts.
- I do not sell or share your data with anyone.
- I do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

## Information the App Accesses

### Location (GPS)

The App requests "Always" location access in order to provide religious practice features that depend on your geographic position (such as prayer times, direction of prayer, and location-based religious observances).

- Your location is processed entirely on your device.
- Your location is never transmitted to me or to any third party.
- Your location is not retained beyond what is necessary for the App's immediate functionality.

You may revoke location permission at any time through your device settings. Disabling location may limit certain features of the App.

### Notifications

The App requests permission to send notifications for time-sensitive religious reminders. Notifications are generated locally on your device. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

### Screen Controls (Planned)

The App may, in a future version, request permission to manage screen behavior (for example, to prevent screen dimming during certain observances). This permission, if requested, will operate entirely on your device and will not transmit any information.

## Information the App Does NOT Collect

- I do not require or create user accounts.
- I do not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
- I do not collect payment or financial information. If in-app purchases are offered in the future, they will be processed exclusively through Apple's or Google's in-app purchase systems, and I will not have access to your payment details.
- I do not collect user-generated content.
- I do not track you across other apps or websites.
- I do not use analytics, advertising, or crash reporting services at this time.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). I do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not currently integrate with any third-party services, analytics providers, advertising networks, or crash reporting tools. If this changes in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated before any such integration is activated, and where legally required, consent will be requested through an in-app prompt.

## Your Rights

Because I do not collect or store your personal data on any server, there is no server-side data for me to access, correct, or delete. All data remains under your control on your device. You can:

- Revoke any permission at any time through your device settings.
- Uninstall the App, which will remove all App-related data from your device.

Depending on where you live (for example, the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, California, or Israel), you may have additional rights under applicable laws, including the GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and Israel's Privacy Protection Law, 5741-1981. To exercise any such rights or ask questions, contact me at the email address above.

## Data Security

Because your data remains on your device, its security depends on the security of your device. I recommend keeping your device locked with a passcode or biometric lock and keeping your operating system up to date.

## International Users

The Developer is located in Israel. The App does not transfer your data internationally because your data does not leave your device.

## Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above. If the App begins collecting or transmitting any data, I will update this policy and notify users within the App before such collection begins.
